# Neon Breakout

A modern, high-intensity take on the classic Atari Breakout. Built with HTML5 Canvas, CSS3, and Vanilla JavaScript, featuring professional neon aesthetics, dynamic physics, and addictive gameplay.

## üöÄ Features

- **Level Patterns**: Unique brick layouts (Full, Checkerboard, Pyramid, Diamond) that cycle every level.
- **Power-up System**: Collect falling items to activate Multi-Ball, Paddle Expansion, or Mega-Ball effects.
- **Synth Audio Engine**: Custom retro-wave sound effects for all game actions.
- **Neon Aesthetics**: Vibrant, glowing visuals with smooth animations and trail effects.
- **Dynamic Physics**: Enhanced ball-paddle collision logic for better control.
- **Lives System**: Start with 3 lives and try to clear as many levels as possible.
- **High Score Tracking**: Compete against your best score with local persistence.
- **Screen Shake**: High-impact collisions are emphasized with dynamic screen-shake effects.
- **Particle Explosions**: Visual feedback for every brick destroyed.
- **Difficulty Scaling**: The ball speeds up as you progress through levels.

## üéÆ How to Play

### Controls
- **Mouse**: Move your mouse left and right to control the paddle.
- **Keyboard**: Use the **Left Arrow** and **Right Arrow** keys.

### Objective
- Bounce the ball to destroy all bricks in the grid.
- Don't let the ball fall below the paddle!
- Clearing all bricks advances you to the next level.
- You lose a life if the ball falls. The game ends when you run out of lives.
